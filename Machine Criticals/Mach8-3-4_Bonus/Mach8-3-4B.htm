The Machines had a bonus mission in 8.3. It was available upon completion of 8.3.4, but was only accessible if you had at least 100 reputation points and it only worked in richland. It is not included in the archives. The following is a transcript of the dialog of this mission, with bolded text describing actions.

The waypoint leads to the Zion white hallway enterance. Once in the hallways, the marked door will take you into the Zion outpost.

The outpost is filled with hostile Zion npcs.

[Team] Zion Physician: Think you can just come in here, you sons of--

[Team] Zion Kung Fu Grandmaster: For Zoya!

 [Team] Zion Duelist: *CENSORED* Machines are everywhere!

[Team] Zion Kung Fu Grandmaster: Think you can just come in here, you sons of--

[Team] Zion Duelist: For Zoya!

 [Team] Zion SMG Specialist: *CENSORED* Machines are everywhere!

[Team] Zoya: Sometimes... Sometimes I think Agent Pace is kind of hot. Is that...bad?

[Team] Eltigen: Wha-at? Man, how can you even ask that question?
[Team] Eltigen: Oh, snap! See what happens?

[Team] Zoya: Ulp!
[Team] Zion Karate Grandmaster: We've been hacked!

[Team] Zion Destroyer: We've been hacked!

 [Team] Zion Karate Grandmaster: We've been hacked!
 [Team] Zion Aikido Grandmaster: Send in the training sim!

 [Team] Zion SMG Specialist: Send in the training sim!
[Team] Zion Aikido Grandmaster: Send in the training sim!

 The nessecary computer is activated and the mission is accomplished. The training sim can also be activated. I do not have the text for defeating the Sim, assuming it exists.

Exiting the outpost takes you back to Richland, and the mission is over.
